About Me

Hey! I am Arsh, a Computer Science Honours Co-op student at the University of Manitoba. I chose Computer Science for my career because of my interest in Problem solving and Mathematics. I am highly passionate about Computer Graphics, mainly because I get to make cool visuals and translate my knowledge of Physics and Math into renders that everyone can appreciate. I also like to work on web-based systems to feed my curiosity about distributed computing and systems design.


When I am not working on smart stuff, I enjoy listening to podcasts and comedies from my favorite comedians. I also like painting and graphic designing from time to time. If you would like to have a chat, work with me on something fun, or need any comedy content suggestions, feel free to reach out!

Where I have worked

Electronic Arts (EA) is an American video game company, and Frostbite is its in-house game engine. I worked as a Software Developer on Frostbite's Animation team at EA Vancouver for my second work-term.

Ubisoft is a French video game company. I worked as a Tools Programmer at Ubisoft's Winnipeg studio for my first and third work-terms.
